Accommo-Venient was created as solution to help students find off campus residence at their college easier to find. Because coming to think of it, the background of having a place to stay close to campus is a great advantage knowing it’s only a short walk to campus, sometimes we don’t appreciate the proximity to campus until we wake up late for class. There’s nothing quite like the mad dash in your pajamas running for an 8 o’clock lecture, with this one realizes how much impact living close to campus has. Whatever choice you make when deciding where to live, your decision will impact the overall student experience since most of the courses require an adept time management skill. Commuting seems to take a portion of time and also impacts on the study efficiency due to the effects of being fatigued which results in low concentration and low information assimilation .It’s also ideal for people who do not want to stay on campus since not all of us want to be residents on campus because of different reasons which some may include wanting to cook your own desired food and cooking it at flexible times that suit you.

And the truth is that the problem of looking for accommodation becomes hectic after you don’t get the chance to secure accommodation in the school residency. On applying for the accommodation the platform often gets flooded with requests and becomes slow yet it will also be having a very limited number of places available for accommodation, losing the chance of getting to stay on campus often becomes a real problem especially when the semester fully engages you on and starts requiring more and more efficient time management to make you eligible to manage your courses. This often leads to poor and dismal performance when dealing with school work and lectures since the commuting process will take a toll especially with the current situation that usually requires standing in queues or maybe having your commuting buses not available due to them waiting on long fuel queues, this results in late arrivals and losing precious time.

From there we had an aim which was to make the search for accommodation for non-residential students easier with an improved system that also aids you in choosing the right place to suit your needs and saves you from the horrors of having to stay in a place that you don’t want to stay. This gave us the zeal to make a move and help better our college life as students and thus we set out objectives and we wanted to make sure that

  • the student had a place to easily search for available accommodation rather than having to physically commute from place to place looking for a "good place" to stay.
  • accommodation had good detailed information and to try to lessen the unanswered questions and to also lessen ambiguities by increasing the level of being discrete.
  • make sure you have a good look of how your new place to be will actually look like and also how the experience will most likely be from other people's feedback and reviews

Our justification is that not being able to secure a place in the schools residency results in poor time management for most students, since most students will be commuting. Commuting plays a big role in time lost since it takes a fair portion of time going to school and coming from school, it becomes worse with students who have to commute twice to get to school and when coupled with transport issues it becomes even worse. Upon arrival at home most students complain on getting home tired due to the bundled up fatigue they experience as they commute every day. Accommo-vinient aids in helping the students on their search for a good place for residency, since it also cuts the issue of having to travel from home or location out of Harare, so that you can search the houses since it shows you recent pictures of the house you’re searching for and also shows you the house’s geographical location as you’ll be viewing its area and its community .
After all this thought processing and everything we came to a conclusion that we should create a platform that will assist and curb this issue.

Bringing you Accommo-Venient, A service that offers convenience in delivery of its services to those who want to find off campus residence, thus making the whole process of looking for accommodation easy and effortless #convenient accommodation
